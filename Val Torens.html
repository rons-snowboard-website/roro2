Val Torens

Val Thorens: The Roof of the Alps Awaits

Val Thorens is a ski resort located in the Tarentaise Valley of the French Alps. It boasts the title of the highest ski resort in Europe, with its base elevation at a cool 2,300 meters (7,545 ft) and the highest ski lift reaching a staggering 3,230 meters (10,600 ft). This lofty position translates to a long ski season, typically lasting from late November to early May, and a near guarantee of excellent snow conditions.

More Than Just Altitude:

Beyond its impressive height, Val Thorens offers a variety of features that make it a popular destination for skiers and snowboarders of all levels:

  • Extensive Terrain: The resort boasts over 600 kilometers of ski slopes, making it part of the largest interconnected ski area in the world, Les 3 Vallées (The 3 Valleys). This vast network gives skiers access to a seemingly endless variety of terrain, from gentle beginner slopes to challenging black diamond runs.

  • Something for Everyone: Whether you're a seasoned skier carving down long descents, a family looking for gentle slopes for the kids, or a snowboarder seeking freestyle parks, Val Thorens caters to all interests.

  • Après-Ski:The resort is known for its lively après-ski scene, with numerous bars, restaurants, and nightclubs offering entertainment throughout the day and night.

  • Modern Amenities: Val Thorens offers excellent infrastructure, including modern ski lifts, comfortable accommodations ranging from budget-friendly options to luxurious hotels, and a variety of shops and restaurants to cater to all needs.

  • Family-Friendly: The resort provides numerous amenities and activities specifically geared towards families, including ski schools with qualified instructors for children, childcare facilities, and dedicated beginner areas.

    • Beyond Skiing:

    While skiing and snowboarding are the main attractions, Val Thorens offers other activities to enjoy during your winter vacation:

  • Snowshoeing: Explore the stunning winter landscapes on a guided snowshoe tour.

  • Paragliding: Experience the thrill of soaring above the mountains with a paragliding tandem flight.

  • Ice Skating: Enjoy a leisurely skate on the outdoor ice rink.

  • Relaxation: Unwind at the spa after a day on the slopes, offering massages, saunas, and other wellness treatments.

    Planning Your Trip:

  • Accessibility: Val Thorens is easily accessible from various airports in France and Switzerland, with shuttle services connecting to the resort.

  • Accommodation: Book your accommodation well in advance, especially during peak season.

  • Lift Tickets: Purchase your lift tickets online or at the resort to avoid long queues.

  • Ski Rentals: Numerous ski rental shops offer a wide range of equipment for all levels.

    Val Thorens, with its high altitude, extensive terrain, lively atmosphere, and diverse offerings, promises an unforgettable ski holiday experience for all types of winter sports enthusiasts.
